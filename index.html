Retro Arcade

A collection of classic and addictive browser games.

🐍 Emoji Snake

Grow your snake by eating food, but don't hit the walls!

🧱 Tetris

Fit the falling blocks together to clear lines.

💥 Breakout

Use the paddle to break all the bricks with the ball.

🏓 Pong

The original arcade classic. Don't miss the ball!

🐦 Flappy Bird

Navigate the bird through the pipes. Harder than it looks!

💣 Minesweeper

Clear the board without detonating any hidden mines.
